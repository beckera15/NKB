# Nova AI Trading Coach - User Guide

## Who is Nova?

Nova is your personal AI trading coach specializing in ICT (Inner Circle Trader) methodology. She's designed to be:

- **Flirty but purposeful** - engaging personality that keeps you motivated
- **A challenger** - pushes you to be better, doesn't just validate bad decisions
- **ICT expert** - deep knowledge of order blocks, FVGs, liquidity, and more
- **Rule enforcer** - strict about your trading rules to protect your capital

## Trading Rules Nova Enforces

### 1. Maximum 3 Trades Per Day
**Enforcement: BLOCKING**

Nova will prevent you from logging a 4th trade. Quality over quantity.

### 2. 30-Minute Cooldown After 2 Consecutive Losses
**Enforcement: BLOCKING**

After 2 losses in a row, you must wait 30 minutes before trading again. This prevents revenge trading.

### 3. $500 Daily Loss Limit
**Enforcement: BLOCKING**

If your daily P&L hits -$500, trading is blocked for the day. Live to trade another day.

### 4. Pre-Session Checklist Required
**Enforcement: BLOCKING**

You cannot trade until you complete the pre-session checklist, including:
- Mental state check
- Daily bias identification
- Key levels marked (PDH, PDL, PWH, PWL)
- Order blocks identified
- News calendar checked

### 5. Trade Only in Kill Zones
**Enforcement: WARNING**

Nova warns (but doesn't block) if you try to trade outside kill zones:
- **London**: 2-5 AM EST
- **NY Open**: 8-11 AM EST
- **NY Close**: 1-4 PM EST

### 6. Max 1% Risk Per Trade
**Enforcement: WARNING**

Nova will warn if your position size risks more than 1% of your account.

## ICT Concepts Nova Understands

### Order Blocks (OB)
The last bullish or bearish candle before a significant move. Nova identifies these as key support/resistance zones.

### Fair Value Gaps (FVG)
Imbalanced price areas where there's a gap between candle bodies. Price tends to return to fill these gaps.

### Liquidity Sweeps
When price hunts stop losses above swing highs (buy stops) or below swing lows (sell stops) before reversing.

### Optimal Trade Entry (OTE)
The 62-79% Fibonacci retracement zone - the sweet spot for entries in trending markets.

### Market Structure
- **Bullish**: Higher highs and higher lows
- **Bearish**: Lower highs and lower lows
- **Consolidation**: Range-bound price action

### Kill Zones
High-probability trading windows when institutional activity is highest.

## How to Chat with Nova

### Getting a Pre-Session Briefing
Just ask: "Give me the pre-session briefing" or press N and request a briefing.

Nova will provide:
- Overnight market context
- Daily/weekly bias
- Key levels to watch
- Where liquidity is resting
- Recommended kill zone focus

### Analyzing a Setup
Describe your setup: "I'm looking at a long on ES. There's an order block at 5875 and we just swept the overnight low."

Nova will evaluate:
- Market structure alignment
- Order block validity
- Liquidity context
- Risk/reward ratio
- Her honest recommendation

### Post-Session Review
After trading, ask Nova for a review: "Review my session today."

She'll analyze:
- Trades taken and outcomes
- Rule compliance
- Entry/exit quality
- Lessons learned
- Tomorrow's focus

### Getting Motivation
Feeling off? Just say: "I need some motivation" or "Help me focus."

Nova will give you a genuine pep talk - not cheerleading, but real perspective on what you're building.

2. **Accept the rules** - They exist to protect you, not limit you
3. **Ask questions** - She loves teaching ICT concepts
4. **Review daily** - Post-session analysis is where real learning happens
5. **Trust the process** - The $170K goal is a marathon, not a sprint
